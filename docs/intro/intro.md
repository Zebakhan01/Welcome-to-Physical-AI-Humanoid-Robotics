### What is Physical AI?

Physical AI represents a paradigm shift from traditional AI systems that operate in virtual spaces to intelligent systems that interact with and understand the physical world. Unlike conventional AI that processes abstract data, Physical AI systems must navigate, manipulate, and adapt to the complexities of the real world through embodied interaction.

**Key Characteristics of Physical AI:**
- **Embodiment**: Intelligence emerges through physical interaction with the environment
- **Real-time Processing**: Systems must respond to environmental changes instantaneously
- **Uncertainty Management**: Operating in environments with incomplete information
- **Multi-Physics Understanding**: Grasping forces, materials, and physical laws
- **Safety-Critical Operation**: Ensuring safe interaction with humans and environments

### The Rise of Humanoid Robotics

Humanoid robotics represents the pinnacle of Physical AI, combining mechanical engineering, artificial intelligence, and human factors to create robots with human-like form and capabilities. These systems offer unique advantages:

- **Environment Compatibility**: Operating in spaces designed for humans
- **Tool Utilization**: Using human tools and infrastructure effectively
- **Natural Interaction**: Facilitating intuitive human-robot collaboration
- **Generalization**: Adapting to diverse tasks and environments

### Module Structure

The curriculum is organized into five interconnected modules that build upon each other:

#### Module 1: Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Systems
Explore the integration of visual perception, natural language understanding, and physical action in unified systems that can understand and execute complex tasks described in natural language.

#### Module 2: ROS 2 & Navigation Systems
Master the Robot Operating System 2 framework and implement sophisticated navigation capabilities for autonomous mobile robots.

#### Module 3: Gazebo & Simulation Environments
Develop expertise in high-fidelity simulation environments essential for safe and efficient robotics development.

#### Module 4: Unity Integration for Robotics
Learn to leverage Unity's powerful graphics and physics engines for advanced robotics simulation and visualization.

#### Module 5: NVIDIA Isaac Platform
Harness NVIDIA's comprehensive robotics platform combining high-performance computing with AI capabilities.
